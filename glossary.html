Words Used Throughout This Webpage:

  • Game Theory: The study of the analysis or strategy used in a competitive game.
  • Socializer: A socializer is a gamer type in which they enjoy playing to be social.
  • Achiever: An achiever is one who uses direction and motivation as a way to reach the end goal which is usually a prize of some kind.
  • Explorer: An explorer is one who likes to look around and even find parts of the game that are undiscovered.
  • Killer: A killer is a person who likes to dominate and conquer the game.
  • Pseudocode: A notation that resembles a programming language which is often used in program design.
  • Flowchart: A diagram that represents a flow or process usually in a step-by-step format.
