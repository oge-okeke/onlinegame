Social Issue to be Addressed

The social issue that we wanted to address via game is littering. I want to address this social issue because it is still an ongoing issue in America and all over the world and one of the causes of the worldly disasters that we face such as pollution, climate change, and more. To track the effectiveness, this could be measured by seeing how much trash could be collected within a certain time frame while trash regenerates as the screen becomes empty. The impact we will create through this game is to prevent littering from continuing and to make better choices when irresponsibly and carelessly disposing of trash. Another way to track the effectiveness of our game: before we say the name of the game and give the instructions, we give the players a form to answer regarding littering and then while they play the game, facts will pop up that they can read and then at the end, we give another form for them to fill in and see how much they learned from it. Then, we will know how much information they gained.
