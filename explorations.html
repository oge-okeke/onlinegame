Explorations in Gaming

Before creating our game, we had to do some exploration which called for a creative idea of a board game. After gaining some inspiration, we dove straight into our video game, but first we needed a format and exact idea on what our character would be doing. After creating that, we decided to each branch off to create pseudocode so that we can form a uniformed idea.

While discovering more about creating a video game, we knew that we wanted it to be fun and interactive. We then derived some of the code from the examples given in class. The created game is called: "Litterbugs." In this game, we are trying to prevent littering from continuing and to make better choices when irresponsibly and carelessly disposing of trash. To measure the impact of the game, before we say the name of the game and give the instructions, we give the players a form to answer regarding littering and then when they play the game, quick facts will print on the screen. After, we will send out a form again to see how much information they gained.
