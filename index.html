Litterbugs

“Terrible air, no fair!”

        

Living in a trash ridden neighborhood sucks and you, yes YOU, are capable of making it better! Pick up bottles, cans, and more while dodging objects, make this neighborhood shine! Keep in mind you have until it gets dark to pick up as much trash as possible, it can be dangerous outside when it's late.
